Welcome to Around the World by Gabriel Georgescu

Around The World by Gabriel Georgescu is a collection of 6,999 unique pictures from 14 countries that live on the Ethereum blockchain. Pictures are taken by Gabriel Georgescu from 2016 to 2021. Every picture is unique and has multiple traits. From country, city and color to photography type.
